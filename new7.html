Summary of Changes - Infographic

This interactive infographic provides a summary of the proposed changes to the HQM scheme manual. It captures the main types of changes to issues in the scheme manual.

Hover over a type of change for issues are affected by it. Alternatively hover over an HQM issue to reveal the types of changes affecting the issue. The height of the bar in an issue indicates the extent of the change for that issue. Please see descriptors below to know what each item listed under Type of Change relates to.

Please note the interactive version of the infographic cannot be viewed in some browsers.

Amended Content: Substantial changes to the criteria, methodology, benchmarks, scope or compliance notes.

Credits Changes: Increase or decrease in the total number of credits available for an issue. In some cases this can relate to a change in benchmarks.

Issue Restructure: Contents of the issue has been reorganised for clarity and to ensure content is appropriate for the section. This can mean restructuring of content within a section (example regrouping of criteria requirements) or across sections (example compliance notes have been moved into the methodology section or the definition section)

New Content: Introduction of new criteria, methodology, checklist, compliance note, benchmark or definition

New Minimum requirement / pre-requisite - Introduction of a new minimum requirements or prerequisite in an issue

Scotland Specific Content - Criteria, methodology, benchmark, compliance note, definition or standard applicable to assessments in Scotland.

Change of Standards - Update or replacement of standards referenced in an issue or introduction of new standards Wales Specific Content- Criteria, methodology, benchmark, compliance note, definition or standard applicable to assessments in Wales.

Wording Tweaks - Minor structural changes, wording changes or deletions to aim, benefit, context, criteria, methodology, compliance notes, definitions, evidence requirements or notes to improve clarity.
